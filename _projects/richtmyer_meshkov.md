We investigate the turbulent mixing induced by the Richtmyer-Meshkov instability through direct numerical simulation. The study reveals the scale-to-scale energy transfer mechanism in the mixing zone, providing fundamental understanding of compressible turbulent mixing.

**Publications:**
- Liu, H. & Xiao, Z. (2016), "Scale-to-scale energy transfer in mixing flow induced by the Richtmyer-Meshkov instability," *Physical Review E*, 93, 053112.
- Wu, J., Liu, H. & Xiao, Z. (2021), "Refined modeling for single-mode cylindrical Richtmyer-Meshkov instability," *Journal of Fluid Mechanics*, 908.
- Wu, J., Liu, H. & Xiao, Z. (2019), "A numerical investigation of Richtmyer-Meshkov instability in spherical geometry," *AAMM*, 11(3), 583-597.
